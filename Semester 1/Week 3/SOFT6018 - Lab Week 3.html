Part 1 - Country Rating Program

Write a program which does the following

  1. Asks the user how many countries they have visited ?
  2. Create a String array which is capable of storing all the countries names.
  3. Populate this array by asking the user to supply the country names one by one.
  4. Now ask the user to grade each country out of 10.(These scores should be stored in an int array)

  5. If for example they entered "Australia", "Spain" and "America" they should be prompted with the following type of question when scoring each country

    • What is your score out of 10 for Australia ?
    • What is your score out of 10 for Spain ?
    • What is your score out of 10 for America ?
  6. Print out the average score for all the countries they have visited. The message should be of the format "You have visited 3 countries and the average score is 7".

NOTE : Your program should work for any number of countries

Sample Output
  • How many countries have you visited ? 3
  • What is the name of country 1 ? Australia
  • What is the name of country 2 ? Spain
  • What is the name of country 3 ? America
  • -----
  • What is your score out of 10 for Australia ?8
  • What is your score out of 10 for Spain ?9
  • What is your score out of 10 for America ?6
  • -----
  • You have visited 3 countries and the average score is 7
